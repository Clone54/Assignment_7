KeenKeeper

--> KeenKeeper is a personal relationship management tool designed to help you nurture your connections. Browse, tend, and nurture the relationships that matter most with ease.

Features:

Friend Management: Keep a detailed list of your friends with their contact status, goals, and personal bios.
Real-time Timeline: Log your interactions (Calls, Texts, Video calls) and see them instantly in your timeline.

Friendship Analytics: Visualize your interaction habits with beautiful charts to see how you're connecting.

Responsive Design: Fully optimized for mobile, tablet, and desktop screens.

Smart Status Tracking: Automatically categorizes friends as "on-track", "almost due", or "overdue" based on your contact goals.

Technologies Used

React.js: For building the interactive user interface.
React Router: For seamless single-page navigation.
Tailwind CSS: For modern, utility-first styling.
Lucide React: For beautiful, consistent iconography.
Framer Motion: For smooth animations and transitions.
Recharts: For data visualization and analytics.
React Hot Toast: For elegant, non-intrusive notifications.

How to Use

1. Home Page: View your summary stats and your list of friends.
2. Friend Details: Click on any friend to see their full profile, stats, and log a quick check-in.
3. Timeline: View and filter your history of interactions.
4. Stats: Check your friendship analytics to see your most common interaction types.
